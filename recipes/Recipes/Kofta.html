Kofta

Source: ethanchlebowski.com

Ingredients

Meat Mixture

454 grams (1 lb) ground meat [Lamb, beef, chicken]

7 g salt (1.5% by meat weight)

1/4 onion, grated

1 clove garlic, grated

Small handful parsley, minced

Large spoonful spice mix

(recipe below)

Kefta Spice Mix

2 spoonfuls ground cumin

1 spoonful chile powder

1 spoonful paprika

1 spoonful oregano

1/4 spoonful ground cinnamon

Yogurt sauce

Yogurt

Lemon zest

Lemon juice

Garlic clove

Salt to taste

Black pepper

Sumac

Serve with:

Lavash

Tomato

Arugula (or lettuce)

Pickled onion

Feta

Directions

Mix, form, cook, enjoy.
