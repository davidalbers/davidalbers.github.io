Salmon With Cream Sauce

Ingredients

Ingredients:


STEP: 1 (Spices)


2 Tablespoon Butter Softened


2 Tablespoon Lemon Juice


2 Tablespoon All Purpose Flour


1/4 Teaspoon Salt


1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper, Grounded


2 Cloves Garlic, Minced


1 Tablespoon Honey


STEP: 2 (Pan Sear fish)


2 (120gm) Each Salmon Fillet


2 Tablespoon Butter


STEP: 3 (Sauce)


1/4 Cup Cream


1/4 Teaspoon Black Pepper, Grounded


1/8 Teaspoon Salt


1 Clove Garlic, Minced


1 Tablespoon Parsley, Flat leaves, diced


1/4 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning


1 Tablespoon Lemon Juice


1 Tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil


STEP: 4 (Sautéed Mushrooms with Spinach)


1 Tablespoon Butter Softened


2 Cups Mushroom, Cut Into 4


2 Cloves Garlic Minced


4 Cups Baby Spinach


1 Cup Tomatoes , Chopped


1/8 Teaspoon Salt


1/4 Teaspoon Black Pepper, Grounded

Description

Directions:

STEP: 1 (Spices)

In a bowl add step 1 ingredients and mix it well.

STEP: 2 (Pan Sear fish)

Take salmon fillet and add 2 tablespoons of step 1 mixture in each fillet.

Cook both on medium heat, flesh side for 2 minutes and skin side 10 minutes.

STEP: 3 (Sauce)

In a pan add step 3 ingredients and cook for 3 minutes on medium heat.

STEP: 4 (Sautéed Mushrooms with Spinach)

In a pan add the following ingredients: softened butter , mushroom, minced garlic sauté for 5 minutes on medium heat.

After that add baby spinach, tomatoes, salt, black pepper sauté for 2 minutes on medium heat.
